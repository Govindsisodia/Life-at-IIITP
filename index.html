SPORTS CLUB

"Sports", club is committed to provide a healthy sporting habit among the students. It helps to learn teamwork, coordination among diverse cultural & ethnic groups and mainly infuses discipline & instills the value system in one individual. The club acknowledges that leisure as well as intensive activities are vital to keep one’s mind fresh and healthy. Sports importance for people in the country is really impresssive and impeccable and also it helps developing a sense of natural competitiveness and goal-oriented personal approach.

ECLECTIC CLUB

“Eclectic”, the literary and debating society of Indian Institute of Information Technology Pune has seen all the heights and depths in the many years of its existence.What started just as a club for literary enthusiasts in the institute to come together and colloquially “hang-out” has now become a professional society with a widespread and a respected community with a social responsibility, credit to which would definitely go to our members who have represented us on various national platforms.

VANITY CREW

"Vanity Crew", acts as a platform to help you explore your artistic talents, boost your confidence and give meaning to the love you hold towards dance. From organising flash mobs, to dance workshops, and not to mention, dynamic dance performances at college events, it is exactly the place to showcase your talents and get the chance to confidently exhibit your hilarious moves. Vanity Crew is not being just occasionally active, but constant practice, aiming perfection.

ABHINAY

"Abhinay", the dramatics club of IIIT Pune, is the perfect platform to enhance, embrace and nourish the ‘Nautanki’ factor in you.A club where you get to explore the fundamentals of acting, play theater games and even get to perform original plays by local, young playwrights.Abhinay was formed by the very first batch of IIIT Pune by the vision of creating, encouraging and enhancing the art of theatre, acting and stage performances.

SAAZ

"Saaz", Club at IIITP ensures that everyone remains in touch with the artistic side of theirs, by creating music spanning across different genres. We never miss participating in any event. From soothing and pacifying solo performances to zestful, rocking band performances, we got it all.With the technical proficiency and music knowledge of the club members, willing performers get to express their music in the most impactful,impressive and engaging way.
